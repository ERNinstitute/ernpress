About ERN Press

ERN Press is the scholarly publishing arm of the Experience-Related Neuroplasticity Institute.

ERN Press provides a publishing home for rigorous open-access scholarship in areas where experience, evidence, and change intersect.

Mission. To support scholarly publishing that is precise, accessible, interdisciplinary, and responsible in its treatment of evidence.

Publisher Orientation

ERN Press is built for scholarship that benefits from disciplinary depth and cross-field readability. Its publishing program emphasizes cross-disciplinary integration, theoretical clarity, methodological care, transparent reporting, and careful interpretation of complex evidence.

Scholarly Values

  • Clear questions and defensible claims
  • Transparent methods and interpretation
  • Responsible treatment of uncertainty
  • Respect for cumulative evidence

Publishing Values

  • Editorial independence
  • Open-access dissemination
  • Accessible presentation of specialized work
  • Durable, sustainable scholarly communication

Leadership

John G. Grundy, Ph.D., FPsyS

Director, ERN Press and Experience-Related Neuroplasticity Institute

Dr. Grundy directs ERN Press as part of the Institute’s broader work supporting scholarship on experience, adaptation, methods, and evidence evaluation.
